About FLAG

Faruqi Legal Action Group (FLAG) is built on a commitment to strategic, practical, and results-driven advocacy. With a diverse foundation in litigation, negotiation, toxic exposure matters, business law, contract drafting, and dispute resolution, FLAG delivers competent, adaptable, and professional representation.

Our experience spans complex legal issues and everyday matters alike — offering individuals and businesses clear guidance and strong advocacy across a wide spectrum of legal needs.
