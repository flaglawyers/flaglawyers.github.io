About FLAG

Faruqi Legal Action Group (FLAG) is built around a simple principle: strong work product and clear strategy drive better outcomes. We approach each matter with disciplined analysis, meticulous documentation, and practical advocacy—whether the need is a contract, a negotiation plan, dispute escalation, or case organization for complex issues.

Clients come to FLAG for professional communication, careful issue-spotting, and a structured path forward. We prioritize clarity: the facts, the options, the risks, and the next best step.

How We Work
Tight timelines, organized files, clear writing, and strategic negotiation—built for efficiency and leverage.
What You Can Expect
Professional intake, transparent steps, and documents drafted to be used—not “template language.”
Focus
Contracts, disputes, liability planning, insurance issues, and evidence-forward evaluation of complex matters.

Services

FLAG offers practical legal services designed for real-world needs—clear documents, disciplined negotiation, and well-organized case strategy. Click a service to view details.

Broad legal support for everyday personal and business matters—focused on issue-spotting, document readiness, and practical resolution. If your situation touches contracts, disputes, liability exposure, or business operations, FLAG can typically help or coordinate the right next step.
Agreements built to perform in the real world: scope, payment protections, deliverables, deadlines, change orders, termination, remedies, indemnity, and dispute provisions. FLAG emphasizes clear language and enforceable risk allocation—so your contract prevents problems instead of creating them.
Formation-stage and operating agreements designed to reduce friction: roles, ownership, decision rights, deadlock resolution, buy-sell triggers, confidentiality, and exit strategy. This is where disciplined drafting protects relationships and preserves value.
Strong written advocacy and escalation strategy—demand letters, responses, and settlement proposals designed to frame the issue, preserve leverage, and move the other side toward resolution. Tone and record-building are handled with care.
Structured negotiation support: objectives, leverage analysis, concession planning, and scripted communication. FLAG’s approach mirrors how sophisticated disputes are resolved—clear ranges, controlled messaging, and disciplined decision-making.
Mediation-ready case files: clean chronology, exhibits organized, damages and risks assessed, and a settlement plan that anticipates counter-moves. Prepared parties settle better—because they control the narrative and know the numbers.
Claim support when the process drifts: coverage questions, documentation packages, and dispute strategy. FLAG emphasizes a strong paper trail—policy language, claim history, and communications organized for clarity and leverage.
Practical risk control through contracts and policies: waivers, limitation of liability clauses, indemnity, insurance requirements, and dispute provisions. The goal is straightforward: reduce exposure and improve predictability.
Structured evaluation of facts, fault, damages, and documentation needs. FLAG emphasizes early record-building, medical documentation organization, and a clear theory of responsibility—so decisions are informed and strategic.
Evidence-forward evaluation of exposure narratives: product/source identification, exposure timeline, medical record organization, and responsible-party mapping. FLAG’s process is built for technical fact patterns where organization and causation thinking matter.
For matters that demand structure: chronologies, exhibit organization, issue outlines, and written advocacy that tracks the record. FLAG’s work product is built to look and read like serious legal work—because it is.
